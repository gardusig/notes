## Per-template intent

- `template-dag`: fan-out and delegation trees.
- `template-decision`: branching policy or UX decisions.
- `template-flowchart`: top-down process pipelines.
- `template-sequence`: participant interactions over time.
- `template-stakeholder-flow`: lane-based cross-team flow.
- `template-state`: lifecycle transitions.
- `template-user-journey`: user steps, friction, and recovery.
- `template-domain-taxonomy`: public/human/internal layering.
