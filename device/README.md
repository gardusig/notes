## Recommended template for a device guide

Mirror **[`computer/windows.md`](computer/windows.md)**:

1. **`## Purpose`** — Role of the device, hard constraints (OS, form factor, region/currency if you shop).
2. **`## Summary`** — Short bullets: stack, software, rough spend or decision outcome.
3. **`## Index`** — Anchor links to every major section.
4. **`## Setup & installation`** — First boot, OS tuning, apps, workflows (use `###` subsections).
5. **`## What to buy & price expectations`** — SKUs, tiers, and money guidance when it applies; use a short **Hardware assumptions** stub if there is nothing to buy.

Splitting Purpose and Summary into two `##` blocks (like Windows) or merging into **Purpose & summary** is fine; keep the **information** in both cases.
