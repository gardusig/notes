## 📌 4. Recipe

1. Preheat oven per hub.
2. Stretch dough; sauce + oregano.
3. **Drain tuna well**; flake evenly (avoid water bombs).
4. Cheese on top (or tuna under cheese if you prefer less dry tuna surface — pick one).
5. Bake **8–14 min** until crust is good and cheese melted.
