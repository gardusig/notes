- **Cutting board** and **chef’s knife** for onion and garlic.

- Large spoon + bowl — see [Eat — conventions](../README.md#conventions).

## 📌 4. Recipe

### 🥗 Ingredients

| Item | Type | Qty | Weight (g) | kcal (approx.) |
| --- | --- | --- | ---: | ---: |
| Pasta (dry) | carb | 500 g | 500 | ~1,750 |
| Ground beef | protein | 500 g | 500 | ~875 |
| Onion | aromatic | 1 medium | 150 | ~60 |
| Garlic | aromatic | 2–3 cloves | 12 | ~15 |
| Tomato (fresh) or passata | vegetable / liquid | 2 medium *or* ~350 g sauce | 300 | ~55–120 |
| Salt, pepper | seasoning | to taste | — | ~0 |

*Tomato line: fresh two medium ~300 g ~55 kcal; jar passata ~350 g ~120 kcal — pick one band for your total.*

### 📝 Instructions

1. Cook pasta.
2. Brown beef.
3. Add onion + garlic.
4. Add tomatoes/sauce.
5. Simmer 10–15 min.
6. Combine with pasta.
