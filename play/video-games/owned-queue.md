# Queue — in progress & owned

Single place for games you **own**: what is **active** right now and what is **not started** (backlog). **Wishlist / not owned** stays in **[to-buy.md](to-buy.md)**. **Finished** rows move to **`completed/`** using **Archive shelf** / **Done →**.

**How to use with [genre-situations.md](genre-situations.md):** pick a **mood or situation** there, then come back here and choose a row whose **Kind** matches—ideally keep **at least one owned “play next” per domain** (`2d`, `3d`, `emulation`) across **In progress** + **Owned** combined so you always have a slot to rotate into (add an emu ROM backlog row when the **emulation** column is empty).

## In progress

Everything you are **playing now** (any presentation style). When you **finish**, append the row to the right file under **`completed/`** per **Done →**, then remove it from this section.

**Legend**

- **Achievements ~:** optional (`12/40`, `Platinum`, `—`, etc.).
- **My playtime ~:** logged hours or rough progress.
- **HLTB ~:** anchors from [HowLongToBeat](https://howlongtobeat.com/).
- **Done →:** `completed/2d.md` \| `completed/3d.md` \| `completed/emulation.md`.
