## 📌 4. Recipe

1. Preheat oven per hub.
2. Stretch dough; **sauce** + **oregano**.
3. **Cheese** evenly; optional **parmesan** finish after bake.
4. Bake **8–14 min** until crust and cheese look right.
5. Optional **oregano** on top after bake.
