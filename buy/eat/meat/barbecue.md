## 📌 3. Prerequisites

- **Kettle grill** or similar with **grate**; **charcoal**; **chimney starter** (recommended).
- **Long tongs**; **heat gloves**; **instant-read thermometer** (optional but good for thick filé).
- **Tray** for rested meat; **sharp knife** for slicing against the grain.

**Fire**

- **Two-zone setup:** one side with **embers**, one **cooler** for holding / indirect if flare-ups.
- **Food safety:** thick cuts → use **internal temp** targets you trust for beef doneness.

---

## 📌 4. Recipe

1. Light charcoal (chimney until **ashed-over**).
2. Bank coals for **two zones**; preheat grate.
3. **Pat meat dry**; salt **close** to cook time (not hours ahead on lean filé unless dry-brining on purpose).
4. Sear over **hot** side → move to cooler side to finish if needed.
5. **Rest** **5–10 min** before slicing.
6. Slice **against the grain**; finish salt if under-seasoned.

---

## 📌 5. Calories and recommendations

- **Lean filé:** ~**250 kcal / 100 g** raw ballpark before cooking loss; **ribeye** runs higher from fat.
- **Charcoal vs taste:** charcoal adds aroma; **ventilation** matters for smoke exposure.
