# 2D games — completed

**Legend**

- **HLTB main / HLTB 100%:** rough community-style times (main story vs completionist). Confirm on [HowLongToBeat](https://howlongtobeat.com/)—numbers move as more people submit.
- **Difficulty:** coarse **typical** bar for skill and friction (not official). Your mileage varies.
- **Status ~ my *~*:** your **state** and **approximate hours** in one cell. Examples: **`100% ~ 84h`** when done; **`Std ~ —`** when you cleared a bar but did not log time.
- **Kind:** short **gameplay** tag (tower defense, find-things, Metroidvania, etc.)—your own labels; not a canonical store genre list.

Active play and owned backlog live in **[owned-queue.md](../owned-queue.md)**. Wishlist lives in **[to-buy.md](../to-buy.md)**.

Links use Steam store pages where available; confirm purchase availability in your region on Steam.

## Notes on difficulty vs 100% time

- **Roguelikes / precision platformers:** **100%** on HLTB is often **mastery time**, not “content volume”—difficulty and hours decouple.

When a row feels wrong for *your* copy (edition, DLC, platform), override the HLTB columns after checking the exact game name on HLTB.
