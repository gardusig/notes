## Decision framework when emotional

1. **Name the state** (tired, angry, spun up, grief, panic).  
2. **Delay irreversible** actions — money, legal, bridges, messages you cannot unsend.  
3. **Shrink the next step** to one concrete action: water, walk, shower, sleep, call one trusted person, or professional triage if health is in question.  
4. If still unstable, **repeat stabilization** before revisiting the decision; use [risk-tiers.md](risk-tiers.md) when something **new** is on the table.
