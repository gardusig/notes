## 1. Pasta

### Ingredients

- 500 g pasta
- 500 g ground beef
- 1 onion
- 2–3 garlic cloves
- 2 tomatoes or sauce
- Salt + pepper

### Instructions

1. Cook pasta.
2. Brown beef.
3. Add onion + garlic.
4. Add tomatoes/sauce.
5. Simmer 10–15 min.
6. Combine with pasta.
