# Daily practice (bounded habits)

**Principle:** **Speed is not the lever.** The win is **showing up** and playing or practicing **reasonably well** (accuracy over rush). External scores are **lagging indicators** — do not chase them in-session.

**Upstream:** [Routine hub](README.md) · [play/puzzles](../../play/puzzles/README.md)

## Chess

- **Puzzles:** aim for **3× per day**; long-term aim a **stable** puzzle rating via **slow, accurate** sessions (no streak-speed chasing).  
- **Play:** **one** serious or rated **game per day** (after puzzles when possible); track on your platform; optional notes in [maintenance.md](maintenance.md) if you log there.

## Other daily lanes

- **LinkedIn:** daily puzzle streak if you keep it bounded.  
- **Duolingo:** short daily lesson if it stays bounded (same quality-over-rush rule).  

## Puzzle roster (weekly touches)

Chess, then Patches, Zip, Sudoku, Tango, Queens, logic, optional word — aim for **~7 lane touches per week** across the roster; log in [maintenance.md](maintenance.md) only if you use those tables.

## Build vs consume

Bounded human puzzle habit supports cognition; **shipping and deep build** still win discretionary time per [`../README.md#long-arc-execution-tie-in`](../README.md#long-arc-execution-tie-in) and [`../rules/governance.md#long-arc`](../rules/governance.md#long-arc).
