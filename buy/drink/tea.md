## 📌 3. Prerequisites

- **Kettle** or pot to heat water to boil / near-boil.
- **Mug or teapot** for the volume you want.
- **Timer** so herbals do not over-steep.
- **Sachets or infuser** for loose leaf.

---

## 📌 4. Recipe

### 🥗 Ingredients

- One tea **sachet** or ~1 tsp loose leaf per ~200–250 ml water (follow the box).
- Water off boil; delicate greens sometimes **~80–85 °C** if you control temperature.

### 📝 Instructions

1. Heat water.
2. Pre-warm the mug with a splash of hot water; discard.
3. Add tea; pour water; cover if you have a lid.
4. **Steep** — **3–5 min** many herbals; black tea often **3–4 min** unless the pack says otherwise.
5. Remove sachet or strain; drink.

---

## 📌 5. Calories and recommendations

- **Plain tea:** effectively **~0 kcal**; honey, sugar, or bottled mixes change that fast.
- **Caffeine:** true black/green tea has caffeine — stack with [coffee.md](coffee.md) budget.
- **Rotation:** keep a few flavors so boredom does not push you toward sugary drinks.
