## 📌 3. Laundry mode vs spend (rough compare)

Illustrative **BRL / month** bands — replace with your real **loads/mo**, **kWh**, **water**, and **lavanderia** menu prices.

| Mode                                                              | Buy detergent & softener (§2) | Typical extra laundry spend (rough)                                                                              | Notes                                                                                                       |
| ----------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Home washer** (your machine)                                    | **Yes** — keep §2 rows        | **Power + water** often **~R$ 30–120/mo** on top of detergent, depending on tariff, efficiency, and **loads/mo** | Biggest swing is **how many hot cycles** you run; cold washes land lower.                                   |
| **Self-service nearby** (walk-in, **you** bring soap)             | **Yes** — keep §2 rows        | **Machine fee** often **~R$ 12–40 per visit** × visits — e.g. **4 × ~R$ 25 ≈ R$ 100/mo** *plus* §2 bottles       | You pay **both** product and **per cycle** unless the shop sells bundled soap you prefer not to track here. |
| **Included / automated** (auto-dispense, staff wash, all-in rent) | **No** — set §2 to **0**      | Usually **already in condo / service / rent** — track elsewhere if you care                                      | This file only holds **bottles you personally restock**.                                                    |

**Decision shortcut:** if you **never** carry detergent home and **never** fill your own machine’s hopper, you are in the **included / automated** column for shopping purposes — **§2 = 0**.
