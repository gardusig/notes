# 🔁 Wake-up (morning routine)

**Window:** 07:00–08:30  

**Upstream:** [Routine hub](README.md)

## 📌 Sequence (strict order)

1. Morning drink (habit anchor, e.g. juice) + coffee  
2. Medications or supplements **only as prescribed or agreed with a clinician**  
3. Water  
4. Recovery check (sleep, mood, pain — adjust the day if needed)  
5. Plan day setup (top task, calendar skim)  
6. Bathroom  
7. Shower  
8. Hygiene  
9. Stretch  

Then follow work onboarding in [`../rules/priority.md`](../rules/priority.md) for the context you are in (employer vs personal).
