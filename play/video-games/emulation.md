# Emulation & non-Steam catalog

**Legend**

- **Not on Steam:** handheld / legacy titles you play via **emulator** or original hardware—no Steam column. Prefer **Platform** + **Notes** for ROM, cart, or backup status.
- **Sections:** same idea as [2D](2d.md) / [3D](3d.md): completed vs active vs backlog. **Incomplete goals** is for “mostly done” runs where you deliberately skipped a huge grind (e.g. full Pokédex).
- **Status ~ my *~*:** same pattern as Steam tables—**`100% ~ 40h`**, **`Incomplete ~ —`**, etc.
