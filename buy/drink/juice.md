## 📌 3. Prerequisites

**Blender**

- **Blender** with a **large jar** and **strong motor** (dense greens + frozen fruit + **2 scoops** whey load the motor — same as your usual max).

**Prep (optional)**

- **Cutting board** and **chef’s knife** if you trim beet.
- **Plastic peeler** (Y-style) if you peel beet instead of scrubbing.

**Serving**

- Glass or shaker bottle to go.

## 📌 4. Recipe

### 🥗 Ingredients

| Item | Type | Qty | Weight (g) | kcal (approx.) |
| --- | --- | --- | ---: | ---: |
| Spinach | vegetable | 1 handful | 50 | ~12 |
| Kale | vegetable | 1 handful | 50 | ~17 |
| Beet (small, peeled) | vegetable | 1 | 50 | ~22 |
| Frozen fruit (e.g. acerola) | fruit | 1 cup | 140 | ~80 |
| Cold water | liquid | ~350 ml | 350 | 0 |
| **Dark Lab Isolate Protein Fuse** | protein | **2 scoops** | ~60–70 *typ.* | *use tub label* (~**80–110 kcal**/scoop typical for isolates) |

### 📝 Instructions

1. Wash all ingredients.
2. Add water first, then **spinach, kale, beet, frozen fruit** into blender.
3. Blend until smooth (**base juice**).
4. Add **2 level scoops Dark Lab Isolate Protein Fuse** (pack scoop); **short blend** or shake in a bottle to limit foam.
5. Adjust water if needed.
6. Optional: strain (usually skip if you want the whey in).
7. Drink immediately.

## 📌 5. Calories and recommendations

**Totals**

- **Base juice only:** ~**130–200 kcal** (fruit sweetness drives the band).
- **With 2 scoops Dark Lab Isolate Protein Fuse:** add **exactly** what the **tub label** says for **2× your scoop** (protein + kcal per serving). **Ballpark** for many isolates: **~160–240 kcal** from the two scoops → **~290–440 kcal** total glass (wide until you pin the label).

**Whey in this recipe**

- **Default:** **2 scoops** **Dark Lab Isolate Protein Fuse** after the base blend (steps §4) — **isolate** usually **lower sugar** than heavily flavored whey; still read **added sugar** and **artificial sweeteners** on your batch.
- If this glass is **not** your only whey that day, count **other shakes** toward your daily protein / kcal plan.
- Tub restock cadence: prorate in §2; generic pantry rows: [eat/pantry.md](../eat/pantry.md).

**Large fruit**

- For whole **pineapple / watermelon / melon**, a **large serrated knife** often beats a chef’s knife; see [Eat — conventions](../eat/README.md#conventions) when this drink pairs with a meal.
