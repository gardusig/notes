# 🥫 Pantry rhythm (online / slow movers)

Shelf-stable items you **restock on a cadence**, not tied to a single meal cook. **Rough BRL**; packs and promos vary. **`Packs / month`** = burn rate at your habit — slow lines use **&lt; 1**.

**Cadence:** ~**2 pantry-style orders / month** (biweekly-ish) if you align to this model.
