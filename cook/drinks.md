## 💚 1. Detox Juice

### 🥗 Ingredients

- 1 handful spinach
- 1 handful kale
- 1 small beet, peeled and chopped
- 1 cup frozen fruit (such as acerola)
- 300–400 ml cold water

### 🔧 Equipment

- **Blender** with a **large jar** and a **strong motor** (high wattage): dense leafy greens, frozen fruit, and optional whey need volume and torque. See [Kitchen tools — Blender & coffee](tools.md#7-blender--coffee).

### 📝 Instructions

1. Wash all ingredients.
2. Add water first, then everything else into blender.
3. Blend until smooth.
4. Adjust water if needed.
5. Optional: strain.
6. Drink immediately.

### ➕ Optional: whey protein

- Up to **~2 scoops whey per day** total if this drink is your main whey window (split across meals if you prefer).
- Add after the base blend; finish with a **short extra blend** or shake in a bottle to limit foam.
- **Vanilla flavor:** ordering notes and label checks live under [E-commerce — Vanilla whey protein](../buy/ecommerce.md#vanilla-whey-protein).

### 🔢 Calories (rough)

- **Juice base** (spinach, kale, beet, ~1 cup frozen fruit, water): often about **~150–350 kcal** depending on fruit and portions; more beet or sweeter fruit pushes the band up.
- **+ ~2 scoops flavored whey:** many vanillas run **~100–130 kcal per scoop** → **~200–260 kcal** for two scoops; **read your bag** (scoop packing, rounding, and formula all move the number).
- **Total:** add the two bands. Flavored whey can add **meaningful sugar** beyond protein alone—use the nutrition label when you care about precision.

## ☕ 2. Filter coffee

### 📌 Supplies

- **Ground coffee** for filter brewing (grind matches your pour habit)
- **Paper filter** sized for your **holder** (basket / #2 / #4, etc.)
- **Hot water** — just off boil is fine; slower pours can use slightly cooler water

### 📝 Instructions

1. Seat the **paper filter in the holder over the mug** (same basket geometry many small drip machines use; a **full electric machine is optional** and often unnecessary for one cup).
2. Add coffee; optionally **bloom** with a little water, wait a few seconds.
3. Pour the rest in stages to your target volume; let drain.
4. Discard filter and grounds; rinse the holder if needed.

### 📌 Tools & shopping

- **Holder**, **filters**, and a **scoop or scale** for repeatable doses: [Kitchen tools — Blender & coffee](tools.md#7-blender--coffee).
