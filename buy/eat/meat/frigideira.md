## 📌 3. Prerequisites

- **Heavy skillet** (cast iron or thick tri-ply) + **vent hood** (smoke is real).
- **Oven-safe pan** if you oven-finish.
- **Tongs**; **instant-read thermometer** optional.

---

## 📌 4. Recipe

1. **Dry** steak surface; **salt** (timing per your habit — many salt right before sear for filé).
2. Pan **ripping hot**; add **thin** oil film.
3. Lay steak; **press lightly** for even contact first minute.
4. **Flip** on a cadence you like (not too often) until **crust** forms.
5. Optional **baste:** reduce heat slightly; foaming butter + garlic + herbs; spoon over steak briefly.
6. Optional **oven finish** for thick cuts after sear.
7. **Rest** on a rack or board **5+ min**; slice against grain.
