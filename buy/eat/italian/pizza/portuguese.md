## 📌 3. Prerequisites

- **Oven** — **required**; tray or stone bakes inside it — see [Pizza hub](README.md) for temperature, tray size, dough and sauce rules.
- **Rolling pin** (or hand-stretch skill).
- **Peel** optional if using stone.

---

## 📌 4. Recipe

1. Preheat oven **max sensible pizza temp** (see hub).
2. Stretch **dough** on a **lightly oiled** tray or floured peel.
3. **Sauce** thinly; **oregano** on sauce.
4. Scatter **onion** + **ham**; cover with **cheese**.
5. Bake until crust **blisters** and cheese **bubbles** (~**8–14 min** — depends on oven).
6. **Eggs:** crack on top **mid-bake** (~4 min remaining) or **late** if you like runnier yolks; return to oven until whites set.
7. Rest **2–3 min**; slice.
