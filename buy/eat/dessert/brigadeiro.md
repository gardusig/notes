# 🍫 Brigadeiro (stovetop)

Classic Brazilian **fudge balls**: condensed milk + cocoa + butter, rolled and coated in **chocolate sprinkles**. Small batch, **dense calories** — plan **yield** and **× / month** honestly.

## 📌 3. Prerequisites

- **Heavy saucepan** + **wooden spoon** / heat spatula (stir constantly).
- **Greased plate** or buttered dish to cool the mass.
- **Buttered hands** or gloves for rolling; **small bowl** of sprinkles.

---

## 📌 4. Recipe

1. Combine **condensed milk**, **butter**, **cocoa**, **salt** in the pan.
2. Cook on **medium**, stirring, until **thick** — **plate test:** a line drawn through the pan bottom holds briefly.
3. Pour onto greased surface; cool until you can handle (**not** fully cold or it is hard to roll).
4. Roll **balls**; drop into sprinkles; coat.
5. Chill before serving if you like them firmer.

---

## 📌 5. Calories

- Most kcal are **sugar + fat** from condensed milk and butter.
- **Servings = N balls** → **kcal / ball = total ÷ N**; weigh one batch once to calibrate your scoop.
