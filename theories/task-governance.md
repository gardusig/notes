## Life priority stack (ordered)

1. **Stay alive**
2. **Clarity (wisdom)** — think before acting
3. **Emotional control (patience)** — avoid impulsive mistakes
4. **Health** — enables everything
5. **Money** — buys optionality
6. **Consistency** — reduces chaos
7. **Achievement** — direction
8. **Reflection** — avoid repeated mistakes

## When emotional

If mood is unstable or the day is “on fire,” **triage P0** (health/safety) first, then **one** smallest concrete step on the next allowed task. Do not optimize the whole board while spun up — stabilize, then re-open [`../tasks/priority.md`](../tasks/priority.md).

## Risk tiers

Use this as a **coarse filter** (not legal advice):

- **High stakes** — health, safety, immigration, tax deadlines, anything with hard external dates.
- **Medium stakes** — money rhythm, employer commitments, backups.
- **Lower stakes** — hygiene, comfort, organizing, leisure.

When unsure, bias toward **writing the next step on a board** instead of holding the loop in your head.

## Thoughts vs execution

Conceptual framing only: if something is **not** the next physical step of your current top task and it keeps looping, **externalize** (name it, put it on the right queue), then **return** to the task you already chose. **Which surface and which step** — [`../tasks/README.md#board-map-and-surfaces`](../tasks/README.md#board-map-and-surfaces) and [`../tasks/priority.md`](../tasks/priority.md).

Boards hold the **queue**; `tasks/priority.md` holds **how you pick**.
